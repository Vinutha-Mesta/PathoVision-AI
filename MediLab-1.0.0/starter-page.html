PathoVision AI – Overview

PathoVision AI is an intelligent histopathology system that analyzes tissue images to assist in early-stage cancer detection using deep learning and explainable AI.

  1. Home
  2. Project Overview

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between artificial intelligence and clinical pathology for faster, transparent, and accessible cancer screening.

What We Do

PathoVision AI uses advanced convolutional neural networks (CNNs) like DenseNet and ResNet to analyze histopathological slides. Each prediction is supported by Grad-CAM heatmaps that highlight the regions influencing the decision, ensuring interpretability.

  • Deep learning–based tissue classification
  • Transparent Grad-CAM visualizations
  • Secure cloud infrastructure for image processing
