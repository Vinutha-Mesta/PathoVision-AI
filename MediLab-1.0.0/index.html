About Us

PathoVision AI leverages deep learning to assist pathologists in identifying colon and lung cancer from digital biopsy slides. Our models provide explainable AI insights through Grad-CAM heatmaps.

  • Accurate Image Analysis

    Our neural networks deliver reliable detection of normal and cancerous tissues.

  • Stage-wise Prediction

    Advanced models classify cancer into stages, aiding in personalized treatment planning.

    Grad-CAM overlays highlight regions influencing the model’s diagnosis, improving trust.
